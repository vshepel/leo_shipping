About Us

“Leo-Shipping” company was founded in 2006 by the market leading specialists with over 15-years of experience in logistics

We provide services for sea, on-land and air transportation for almost any routes, constantly expanding the geography of our cargo transportation.

Qualified employees will solve the assigned tasks and develop an optimised scheme of international transportation to ensure continuous control of the goods entrusted to us along the entire route.

Comprehensive services for all types of transportation

Transportation modes

Sea freight

Shipping is organised for:

  • containers
  • out-of-gauge cargo
  • shipload lots
  • small lot cargo
  • dangerous goods

Insurance
and forwarding

To ensure the cargo safety, we offer a full range of freight forwarding services and insurance. We are ready to provide both a full package of services and partial forwarding works.

Road transportation

Our experts provide delivery of products by any means of transport from the warehouse of the manufacturing plant to the client company. You can use the transport provided by our company, or entrust our specialists with the organization of delivery using your vehicles.

Multimodal transportation

We carry out door-to-door transportation of export-import cargo, as well as provide a full range of services for customs clearance of goods at the Baltic, St. Petersburg and Vyborg customs
